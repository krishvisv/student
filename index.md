## Open Coding Society

<a href="https://sip.opencodingsociety.com/" style="text-decoration: none; display: inline-flex; align-items: center; gap: 8px; padding: 12px 18px; border: 2px solid #FA8072; border-radius: 6px; font-weight: 700; color: #FA8072;">

One of my projects as a part of Open Coding Society was a redesign of a volunteer website. My group picked Soroptimist International of Poway because we supported their belief in empowering young women and girls. We brought our ideas to their site to revise it.

## What Matters to Me

My friends, music, and the environment are really important to me. One of my favorite bands is a local band called Citrus Jr. I go to a lot of their concerts and I love local shows. I have amazing friends from across the states, many from a summer program I did at Georgia Tech. Finding time for the people I love always makes me grateful for the life I live and the people I've met. I want to make sure the work I do in the future is as eco-friendly as possible, helping to preserve our future and save the environment.
